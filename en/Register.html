Registering for your Vaccine


Self-registration is now made available through Co-WIN portal by the Govt of India

Login Here

  • 1. Self-register on the CoWIN Website
  • 2. Upload government photo identity or do an AADHAAR authentication.
    The authentication can happen via biometrics, OTP or demographic.
  • 3. Once registered, a date and time will be allocated for vaccination
  • 4. There will be no on the spot registration and only pre-registered beneficiaries will be allowed to proceed for the vaccination.
  • 5. Respective district administration will be responsible for the session management in the CoWIN system. They will approve the beneficiaries for session and siteallocation. CoWIN will have an inbuilt monitoring and reporting mechanism.
